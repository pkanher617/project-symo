Prove:




Help

In this game, you are given a statement created in the previous game, but without any of the English words. You can still see what logical symbols mean by hovering the mouse over them. At the bottom is another statement, which you are trying to prove by using laws of deduction. The buttons at the bottom allow you to use certain logical rules. After clicking on a rule, click on the statement, statements, or parts of statements to which you want to apply the rule. Modus Ponens will take two statements of the form IF 1 2 and 1 and produce statement 2. Conjoin will combine 1 and 2 into AND 1 2. Replace Tautology will use one statement to replace the same statement, within another statement, with T, and Introduce Tautology will perform the opposite, replacing T with a statement. The Introduce Axioms tab allows you to add statements by specifying logical axioms. The 1, 2, 3 boxes will be replaced by statements that you specify in a dialog. The Rewrite Rules tab will give you ways to transform part or all of a statement according to logical equivalence, which is done by clicking toward the beginning of the part of the statement to which the rule applies. Note that new statements can be generated from any other statement, not only the most recent statement. If you create a statement that is not useful in the proof, you may simply ignore it. The game ends when you create a statement that matches the "Prove:" statement.
